# MemeForge

## Core Pitch & Target Audience
Browser-based meme generator: pick a template, add top + bottom text, download. No watermarks, no signup. Audience: social media managers, Discord users, Redditors, and casual users who need fast memes. Trending meme templates drive viral traffic. Platform-specific (works best shared on desktop or mobile as a link).

## Monetization Engine
- **"Premium Template Pack" (Gumroad, $5 one-time)**: 50 exclusive memes (movie frames, sports, celebrity reactions). New pack monthly. 80 sales/mo = $400/mo.
- **"No Watermark Bundle" ($3 one-time)**: Remove default "made with MemeForge" watermark for pro users who want clean exports. 100 sales/mo = $300/mo.
- **Carbon Ads**: Free tier shows small banner. $100-200/mo revenue.

## SA Banking Payout Route
- **Gumroad**: PayPal or Payoneer → SA bank. Both verified SA-compatible.
- **Carbon Ads**: PayPal → SWIFT to SA bank.
